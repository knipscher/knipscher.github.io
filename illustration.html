1 / 8 - Bacteriopets
Bacteriopets was produced by Pipette Kids and developed by Liza Knipscher. It's a game about the microbes that live inside the human gut.
2 / 8 - Viscera
Viscera is a mobile game demo developed to teach anatomy to teens with a weird sense of humor.
3 / 8 - Beet Blox
This virtual reality game was built for the Vive. It aims to teach about the beetle life cycle—egg, larva, pupa, adult—while asking: what if everything we built was made of live beetles?
4 / 8 - Opsonization
Opsonization is a local multiplayer desktop game in which one person plays as a neutrophil spraying antibodies at viruses,
and the other plays as a white blood cell.
Programmer: Liza; Art Director: Stephanie O'Neil; UI: Andrea Schmitz.
5 / 8 - Cuterus
Cuterus. a mobile AR app, aims to make periods and uteruses a little less intimidating.
Programmer: Liza; Art Director: Kelly Cloninger; UI and additional 2D assets: Angela Gao.
